About the company

Human-focused AI for the digital workplace.

AI-Solution is a Sunderland-based start-up helping organisations improve employee support, customer engagement, and product prototyping through practical AI systems.

Mission

Make workplace support faster, clearer, and more measurable.

The company combines AI assistants, structured web systems, and analytics to help teams answer questions, track engagement, and understand what customers need next.

  • Affordable AI prototyping for early product ideas
  • Simple interfaces designed for non-technical users
  • Admin tools that turn customer activity into useful insight
Values

What shapes the product experience.

Secure by design

Protected admin access and responsible data handling are treated as baseline practice.

Usable first

Navigation, page structure, and forms are designed for clarity and confidence.

Measurable progress

Engagement data supports better decisions after launch.
